Campaign

This is used for managing campaigns. Attention Seeker can do CRUD operations on campaigns. Seeker can also fund their campaigns.

Structure

await arkius.campaign.Functions(...parameter, index, gasReq)

Where:

  • await is used for async function.
  • arkius is an instance of the library.
  • campaign is a module of library for Campaign Contract.
  • Functions are the functions of the Campaign Contract in camel case.
  • ...parameter are the Parameters of functions.
  • index is the index of the Multiple logged in accounts. Default is 0.
  • gasReq is the amount of gas Required to execute the transaction. Default value is already given.
