Certification

This is used for managing certifications. Certifier can do CRUD operations on certifications. Members can subscribe to the certifications or certifier can certify the entity.

Structure

await arkius.certification.Functions(...parameter, index, gasReq)

Where:

  • await is used for async function.
  • arkius is an instance of the library.
  • certification is a module of library for Certification Contract.
  • Functions are the functions of the Certification Contract in camel case.
  • ...parameter are the Parameters of functions.
  • index is the index of the Multiple logged in accounts. Default is 0.
  • gasReq is the amount of gas Required to execute the transaction. Default value is already given.
