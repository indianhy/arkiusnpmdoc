Multiwallet

This is used for transferring tokens from campaign to members and stores the campaign funding done by the attention seekers. Once the user clicks on ad, they can claim their token over here.

Structure

await arkius.multiwallet.Functions(...parameter, index, gasReq)

Where:

  • await is used for async function.
  • arkius is an instance of the library.
  • multiwallet is a module of library for Multiwallet Contract.
  • Functions are the functions of the Multiwallet Contract in camel case.
  • ...parameter are the Parameters of functions.
  • index is the index of the Multiple logged in accounts. Default is 0.
  • gasReq is the amount of gas Required to execute the transaction. Default value is already given.
